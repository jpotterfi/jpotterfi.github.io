Matcha 末茶

because composure is found in a cup of green tea.

reagents

  • ¼ teaspoon matcha.
  • 2 ounces hot water, 175°F is ideal.
  • 6 ounces additional hot water or steamed milk of choice.
  • maple syrup, honey, or other sweetener, optional.
  • matcha whisk.

questline

  1. sift matcha lovingly into most stoic mug.
  2. pour in 2 ounces of hot water.
  3. become tea wizard and conjure foam by moving whisk briskly from side to side.
  4. add in remaining 6 ounces hot water or milk. repeat whisk magic, you tea wizard you.
based off of the fine recipe found here: Love & Lemons
